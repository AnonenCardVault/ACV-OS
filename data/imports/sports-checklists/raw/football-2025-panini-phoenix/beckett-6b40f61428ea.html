Jump to the 2025 Panini Phoenix Football checklist and team set lists

With the release of 2025 Panini Phoenix Football, collectors will once again rise up in search of autographs, prime memorabilia relics, and case-hit inserts from the annual chromium brand.

Hobby boxes deliver 12 packs, and collectors can expect to find two autographs, one memorabilia relic, 12 parallels, 12 rookies, and 6 inserts.

2025 Panini Phoenix Football Checklist Overview

The crisply designed base set will feature both veterans and rookies, and collectors can chase 83 total parallels, with Hyper, Lava, Lazer, Mojo, Pandora, Pyramids, Seismic and Winter designs taking center stage in many colors.

Rare parallels include ColorBurst (/10), Dreamweaver (/6) and Celestial (1/1)

Autographed memorabilia cards of rookies take center stage in Panini Phoenix.

Collectors can find Rookie Patch Autographs that were signed at the Rookie Premiere, and first-year players are also featured in Rookie Phenom Jersey Autographs.

Other autographed memorabilia sets are Rising Star Autographs, Phenomenal Duo Jersey Autographs, and Mythical Material Autographs.

Standalone memorabilia sets include Franchise Future Materials and Pandora Patches.

Inserts like Paragon will be easily found for collectors.

But it’s the case-hit SSPs like Color Blast, Genies, and Phoenicians that will draw the biggest crowds.

2025 Panini Phoenix Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 12
Boxes per case: Hobby – 16
Set size: 250 cards
Release date: November 12, 2025
