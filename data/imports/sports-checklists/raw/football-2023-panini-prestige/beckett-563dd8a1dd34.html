Jump to the 2023 Panini Prestige Football checklist and team set lists.

2023 Panini Prestige Football has a checklist that goes big with its checklist. Lots of autographs, plenty of inserts and a base set that has over 20 levels of parallels.

It’s one of the products that gets a heavy retail presence. To that end, there are mega boxes that have four inserts, a trio of serial-numbered parallels, five exclusive Hyper parallels and a dozen rookies. Autographs are every other box. Blasters have five inserts, one numbered parallel, four Diamond parallels not available anywhere else and eight rookies.

2023 Panini Prestige Football Checklist Overview

The main set has 400 cards. The first 300 are a mix of veterans and retired greats. They’re followed by 90 rookies and the brand’s customary 10 short print rookies.

Once again, parallels adopt the Xtra Points theme. These come not just in a mix of colors but a handful of versions that use different background patterns. There are also further Premium versions. All together, base cards have 23 different parallels. Eighteen of these are serial numbered.

Select cards also have Premium Xtra Points Signatures. These have their own Red (/199 or less), Blue (/99 or less), Gold (/50), Pink (/25), Platinum (/10) and Black (1/1) parallels.

Among the standalone autographs is Distinctive Ink. These cards are exclusive to blasters.

Draft Day Signatures and NFL Passport Signatures are among the places to find autographs from the season’s rookie class.

More than a dozen insert sets round out the checklist.

Of these, Alma Maters, Any Given Sunday, For the Record, Franchise Favorites, Heroes, Living Legends, Power House, Prestigious Pros, Time Stamped, and Youth Movement all have several Xtra Points parallels. These come in Red (/399), Blue (/249), Green (/149), Purple (/99), Gold (/75), Orange (/50), Pink (/25), Platinum (/10) and Black (1/1). Many of these also have autographs as well.

Insert sets without parallels are City Limits, Decorated, Human Highlight Film, NFL Drip, Rookie Portraits, Squad, State of the Art, and Throwing Stars.
