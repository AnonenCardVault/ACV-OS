2024 Panini Certified Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2024 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New

Jump to the 2024 Panini Certified Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Certified Football sticks with a lot of the long-running brand’s hallmarks. These include bright parallels, a broad mix of autographs and memorabilia cards and some tough, standout inserts. The cornerstone Freshman Fabric Signatures are also back, landing one per hobby box.

Collectors can also expect another autograph, a pair of memorabilia cards, 10 inserts and three parallels per box as well.

2024 Panini Certified Football Checklist Overview

Those looking to build the full Certified base set are looking at 200 total cards, which is split evenly between rookies and veterans. Parallels include the line’s customary mix of colors including several with Mirror foil. Autographs are also in the mix for select players.

Numbered Freshman Fabric Signatures highlight key rookie standouts with some of the season’s first autograph patch cards.

Other autograph insert sets include Canton Certified and Piece of the Game, which also come with memorabilia pieces.

Standard insert sets adopt several themes, some more common than other. For those looking for the tough chase sets, there’s The Mighty, Night Moves and Rookie Certifications.

2024 Panini Certified Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 16
Set size: 200 cards
Release date (subject to change): December 6, 2024
