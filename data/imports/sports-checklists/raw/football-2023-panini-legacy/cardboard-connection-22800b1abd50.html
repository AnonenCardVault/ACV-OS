2023 Panini Legacy Football marks the 5th consecutive season for the NFL/collegiate hybrid product.

While the Hobby output sticks with two autographs per box, the format drops from 16 packs to 12 packs per box. The packs still have eight cards.

See also: 2022 Panini Legacy Football Cards

2023 Panini Legacy Football Base / Insert

The 2023 Panini Legacy Football checklist offers a 200-card base set with 100 veterans, 50 rookies, falling two per pack, and 50 legends.

Each Hobby box averages four parallels, four Optichrome cards and two Optichrome Mini cards.

Inserts add another eight cards per box. New choices include Dreamcatchers and Main Attractions.

On top of that, the case-hit Dare to Tear rip cards, numbered to 65 copies or less, highlight rookies and veterans. Each is home to a mystery Optichrome Mini card.

The three-player Past, Present and Future editions are new.

2023 Panini Legacy Football Autographs

Of course, 2023 Panini Legacy Football has many autographs to chase. Future Patch Autographs (#/249 or less) features the 2023 NFL rookie class.

The Legends Autographs insert works in as many as 50 stars, including all-time greats.

Under the Lights Autographs is back, as well, adding all-new Black & White parallels that are capped at 10 copies or less.

The Holographs cards provide another debut. Per Panini, "These holographic cards will be SSP and will be the most sought-after inserts in Legacy."

Release Date: July 28, 2023
Hobby Configuration: 8 cards per pack, 12 packs per box, 24 boxes per case
