2023 Panini Legacy Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2023 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New, New

Jump to the 2023 Panini Legacy Football checklist and team set lists.

Old school meets new school in 2023 Panini Legacy Football. The product takes beats from the hobby’s past and adds some modernity to the mix. This is true not only for the card types and styles but player selection as well. The checklist balances current stars with retired greats and fresh rookies.

Hobby boxes deliver a pair of autographs. Among the other inserts are six total chromium cards, two of which take on mini card dimensions.

2023 Panini Legacy Football Checklist Overview

Legacy’s base set has 200 total cards. Half of those are vets. They’re joined by 50 legends and 50 rookies. Among the parallels are:

  • Red – /299
  • Orange – /199
  • Yellow – /150
  • Green – /100
  • Blue – /50
  • Indigo – /25
  • Violet – /10
  • Black – 1/1

Select cards also have autographs with their own assortment of parallels.

More ink can be found on a few spots of the checklist. For those chasing the season’s draft picks, there’s Futures Patch Autographs. Versions include Silver (/249), Ruby (/100), Sapphire (/25), Yellow Diamond (/10), Diamond (/5) and Platinum (1/1).

Under the Lights Autographs are back for another year. This year, there’s an additional Black and White parallel that tops out at 10 copies each.

Holographs is new to Legacy Football. According to Panini, these are tough pulls.

Rip cards return in the form of Dare to Tear, a concept that’s dates back to the 1990s and Pinnacle brands. The standard-sized cards are case hits and inside each is an exclusive chrome mini card. But in order to get at it and see what level it is, collectors need to tear (and basically wreck) the main card.

Rip cards have three general themes: Veteran Dare to Tear, Rookie Dare to Tear, and multi-player Past, Present and Future Dare to Tear. All have print runs of 65 or fewer.

2023 Panini Legacy Football hobby boxes have eight standard inserts as well. Dreamcatchers and Main Attractions are among the sets.

2023 Panini Legacy Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 8
Packs per box: Hobby – 12
Boxes per case: Hobby – 24
Set size: 200 cards
Release date: July 28, 2023
