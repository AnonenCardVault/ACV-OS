Jump to the 2025 Panini Mosaic Football checklist and team set lists

2025 Panini Mosaic Football marks the first chromium product of the season from the manufacturer, and with it comes the extensive Mosaic Prizms rainbow that collectors have come to love.

Hobby boxes are large, with 150 total cards. Included are two autographs, five Silver Prizms, 15 Mosaic Prizms, and 20 inserts.

2025 Panini Mosaic Football Checklist Overview

Mosaic has become known for its large base sets that cover a lot of ground. From veterans to rookies to Hall of Famers, collectors will find plenty of variety on the 400-card base checklist – and within the parallel rainbow.

Among dozens of Mosaic Prizms will be Tessellation, Gold, and Blue. With 20 total parallels per hobby box, collectors should expect to find two in every pack.

Autographs can be found in sets such as:

  • Rookie Autographs
  • Showtime Signatures
  • In Focus Signatures

Collectors will also be chasing down inserts.

With an average of two inserts per hobby pack, collectors will find many concepts when busting a box, but the biggest chases are the well-known case-hit SSPs like:

  • Micro Mosaic
  • Stained Glass
  • Kaleidoscopic
  • Visionary

Packs per box: Hobby – 10; Mega – 6; Hobby Blaster – 6; No Huddle – 12; Choice – 1; Retail Ornament – 1
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12; Mega – 20; Hobby Blaster – 20; No Huddle – 20; Choice – 20; Retail Ornament – 40
