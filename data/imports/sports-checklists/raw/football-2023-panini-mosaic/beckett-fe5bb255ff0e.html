Jump to the 2023 Panini Mosaic Football checklist and team set lists.

2023 Panini Mosaic Football marks the first full chromium NFL release of the season. The checklist brings with it its familiar and distinct mix of color, patterns, bold designs and mix of inserts. Although not overly focused on traditional hits, hobby boxes still offer a pair of autographs.

2023 Panini Mosaic Football Checklist Overview

The main set for Mosaic has the expected mix of vets and rookies. Like past releases, subsets can also be found such as Hall of Fame and NFL Debut.

Standard cards have Optichrome stock similar to what’s found in sets like Prizm and Select. Silver parallels, which are five per hobby box, deliver the brighter rainbow finish. The remaining parallels, which are 15 per 10-pack hobby box, are where the line’s name comes from. Mosaic parallels get a distinct background pattern that help cards stand out in a different light. Colors here include Red, Blue, Purple, White, Gold and Black. There’s also Tessellation, which is new this year.

Signature themes on the 2023 Mosaic Football checklist include Rookie Autographs, In Focus Signatures and Showtime Signatures. Like other parts of the product, parallels are also available here.

No matter the year or sport, Mosaic has carved out a niche for its inserts. There are lots of themes and most come with bold designs. It’s no different here. Hobby-exclusive sets include Men of Mastery, Splash, Straight Fire and Swagger.

Stained Glass also returns.

2023 Panini Mosaic Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 15
Packs per box: Hobby – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 400 cards
Release date: September 22, 2023
