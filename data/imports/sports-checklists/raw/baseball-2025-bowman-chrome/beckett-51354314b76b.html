2025 Bowman Chrome Baseball Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2025 Baseball Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New, New

Jump to the 2025 Bowman Chrome Baseball checklist and team set lists

2025 Bowman Chrome Baseball is one of Topps’ most anticipated products of the year, and this year’s edition includes some exciting new additions to the annual release that debuted all the way back in 1997.

Hobby boxes guarantee collectors two autographs.

2025 Bowman Chrome Baseball Checklist Overview

The base set will clock in at 100 cards of high-level rookies and veterans, and collectors will also find a separate 100-card checklist of Chrome Prospects, which includes the coveted “1st Bowman” cards. Top names include Andrew Salas of the Marlins and Japanese two-way prospect Shotaro Morii of the Athletics.

Among the dozens of parallels, Topps announced new Refractor colors and styles of:

  • Pulsar
  • Fuchsia
  • Blue Wave
  • Green Wave
  • Black

There will also be 50 Prospects with International Refractors, a concept that debuted in the late 1990s. These cards have backgrounds featuring a player’s native country, and there are also autographed versions.

Insert concepts will include:

  • Meteoric Rise
  • Max Volume
  • Spotlights
  • Ascensions
  • Bowman GPK
  • It Came To The League
  • Melt Mashers
  • Adios

Collectors will also keep their eyes peeled for short-printed rookie cards that are part of the Red Rookie Redemption program, which allows collectors the opportunity to exchange those rookie cards later on, even years down the road, if a player meets certain criteria.

Other base variations include standard image variations, Etched In Glass, Rookie Color Run, and Image Short Prints.

Retrofractors also return, with Tony Perez and Shoeless Joe Jackson on the checklist. These cards give vintage players an official “1st Bowman” card.

In addition to Chrome Prospect and Chrome Rookie Autographs, collectors will search for signatures in the following sets:

  • Prime Choice Signatures
  • Max Volume Autographs
  • Melt Mashers Autographs
  • Adios Autographs
  • Bowman Ascensions
  • Autograph Relics

A late-breaking announcement added GPK Autographs to the product, with some players writing their Garbage Pail Kids name on the card in addition to their signature.

An exciting chase for collectors will be the Bowman Pearl packs, which Topps has inserted about once every 10 cases. These packs are the only place to find limited Pearl Refractors.

Another in-box chase are Variety Packs. These randomly inserted four-card packs include one each of four special Chrome Prospect Refractor variants and could include Prospect Autographs (/5). Special concepts are:

  • Gum Ball Refractor
  • Peanuts Refractor
  • Popcorn Refractor
  • Sunflower Seeds Refractor

2025 Bowman Chrome Baseball cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 10; Breaker Delight – 10
Packs per box: Hobby – 6; Breaker Delight – 1
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12; Breaker Delight – 6
Set size: 100 cards
Release date (subject to change): September 23, 2025

What to expect in a hobby box:

  • Autographs – 2
  • Bowman Chrome Prospect Shimmer Parallel – 1
  • Bowman Chrome Prospect Mini Diamond Refractor – 1
  • Meteoric Rise and Max Volume – 1 Each
  • Melt Masher, It Came to the League and Adios – 2 Each

What to expect in a Breaker Delight box:

  • Autographs – 3
  • Geometric Chrome Prospect Refractors – 3
  • Inserts – 1
