2023 Topps Finest Baseball Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2023 Baseball Cards, Checklists, New
By Ryan Cracknell - July 7, 2023
July 7, 2023
1

Share:

Jump to the 2023 Topps Finest Baseball checklist and team set lists.

Think of 2023 Topps Finest Baseball as the season’s jazz ensemble when it comes to chrome. While Topps Chrome and Bowman Chrome typically take a retread route of recreating and extending existing sets from the same year, Finest follows its own beat with lots of color and bold designs.

Packaged as a pair of six-pack mini boxes, full hobby boxes deliver a pair of autographs. Refractors and a handful of inserts can also be expected.

2023 Topps Finest Baseball Checklist Overview

There’s a couple of levels to the 125-card base set. The first 100 make up the main shell set, highlighting veterans and rookies from across MLB.

Refractors? The checklist has lots of them:

  • Refractors – 1:3 packs
  • Sky Blue Refractors – /300
  • Blue/Aqua Vapor Refractors – /250
  • Purple Refractors – /250
  • Aqua Refractors – /199
  • Aqua Shimmer Refractors – /25
  • Blue Refractors – /150
  • Green Speckle Refractors – /125
  • Green Refractors – /99
  • Lava Green Refractors – /99
  • Rose Gold Refractors – /75
  • Gold Refractors – /50
  • Orange Refractors – /25
  • Red/Black Vapor Refractors – /10
  • Red Refractors – /5
  • Purple/Pink Vapor Refractors – /3
  • Superfractors – 1/1

The final 25 cards make up the Extended Base Set. Available in shorter supply, they also have a smaller parallel rainbow that’s limited to just Gold Refractors (/50), Red Refractors (/5) and Superfractors (1/1).

Autographs and Other Inserts

Finest Rookie Design Variations (1:24 packs) offer up a different look for key first-year players. The layout on these would fit right in with Finest sets from the late 1990s and early ’00s. Refractors (/99), Gold Refractors (/50), Red Refractors (/5) and Superfractors (1/1) are also available for these.

World’s Finest (1:4 packs) draws its inspiration from a 2000-01 Topps Finest Basketball insert set. This time around, there’s more of a change than just sports. Here, the set gets specific, commemorating the 2023 World Baseball Classic.

Finest Headliners (1:6 packs) and Finest Rivalries Die-Cuts (1:48 packs) are among the other insert sets.

All three have a similar mix of Gold Refractor (/50), Red Refractor (/5) and Superfractor (1/1) parallels. Select World’s Finest and Finest Headliners cards also have limited autograph versions with additional Orange Refractors (/25), Red Refractors (/5) and Superfractors (1/1).

Finest Rivalries has dual autographs as well. These have just 10 copies each.

Diamond Finest cards are extremely scarce with just five copies each plus one-of-one Superfractor parallels.

Most of the signatures in 2023 Topps Finest Baseball fall under one of two umbrellas. Finest Autographs carry a similar design as the base set. Finest Rookie Variations Autographs adapt the similar inserts.

Both have mirroring parallels:

  • Blue Refractors – /150
  • Green Refractors – /99
  • Green Wave Refractors – /99
  • Gold Refractors – /50
  • Gold Wave Refractors – /50
  • Orange Refractors – /25
  • Orange Wave Refractors – /25
  • Red Refractors – /5
  • Red Wave Refractors – /5
  • Superfractors – 1/1

Finest Moments return, combining signatures with memorable highlights. The modest parallel lineup for these include Gold Refractors (/50), Orange Refractors (/25), Red Refractors (/5) and Superfractors (1/1).

A Finest Mystery Redemption (/99) features a surprise player and signature.

Finally, Finest Originals Buyback Autographs take old cards from the brand’s past and add players’ penmanship.

2023 Topps Finest Baseball cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 12 (two 6-pack mini boxes)
Boxes per case: Hobby – 8
Set size: 125 cards
Release date: July 7, 2023
