2023 Topps Finest Baseball introduces the next iteration of the long-running MLB card brand.

Finest keeps the Master Hobby box format, with six packs per mini-box. Each mini-box contains one autograph for a total of two signed cards per Master box.

See also: 2022 Topps Finest Baseball Cards

2023 Topps Finest Baseball Base / Refractors

As usual, the base set for 2023 Topps Finest Baseball highlights 100 top names across the league. These players are treated to multiple parallels, but no new versions were announced.

One unannounced option is the Gold Mini-Diamond Refractor (#/50). These appear to be exclusive to Hot Boxes where all cards, including inserts and autographs, come in this form.

Another 25 players are part of the Extended Base Set (1:44 packs), which covers cards #101 to #125. The Refractor rainbow is much smaller for these names.

2023 Topps Finest Baseball Inserts

From there, the 2023 Topps Finest Baseball insert selection mixes old and new styles to form the refreshed lineup.

2000 Topps Finest World's Finest (1:35 packs) showcases 2023 World Baseball Classic (WBC) stars while utilizing the old NBA design.

Other inserts arrive via Finest Headliners (1:4 packs), for past and present superstars, and Finest Rivalries Die-Cuts (1:48 packs), which identifies notable MLB rivals with a linking design.

On top of that, young stars are part of the Finest Rookies Design Variation set, falling at a rate of 1:19 packs.

There's also the ultra-rare Diamond Finest (1:5,101 packs), limited to just five copies, featuring "baseball studs and big-time rookies."

2023 Topps Finest Baseball Autographs

Of course, the 2023 Topps Finest Baseball checklist is boosted by autographs. The main choices include Finest Autographs (1:11 packs) and Finest Rookies Design Variation Autographs (1:174 packs).

Fans can look forward to more signed cards thanks to 2000 Topps Finest World’s Finest Autographs (#'d; 1:772 packs), Finest Headliners Autographs (#/50; 1:8,341 packs) and Finest Moments Autographs (1:127 packs).

The other chase options consist of Finest Mystery Redemption (#/99; 1:6,299 packs), Finest Rivalries Die-Cut Autographs (#/10; 1:7,813 packs) and Finest Originals Buyback Autographs (#/20; 1:2,421 packs).

Release Date: July 7, 2023
Product Configuration: 5 cards per pack, 12 packs per master box (2 minis), 8 master boxes per case
