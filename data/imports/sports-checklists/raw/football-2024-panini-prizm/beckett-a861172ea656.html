2024 Panini Prizm Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2024 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New, New

Jump to the 2024 Panini Prizm Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Prizm Football offers one of the most colorful looks at the season. One of the most engrained modern brands going today, the checklist takes a familiar approach with a few new twists along the way.

Available in several configurations, standard hobby boxes are led by a pair of autographs and more than a dozen Prizms parallels. No Huddle boxes are smaller in size but still deliver one signature and 10 exclusive parallels.

2024 Panini Prizm Football Checklist Overview

The base set sticks strictly to players. This includes a large assortment of rookies that combine to land approximately two per pack.

While lots of products have parallel rainbows, Prizm makes it a defining feature. Each base card comes with dozens of different versions. At the hobby level, ten Prizms in each box are serial-numbered with many exclusives.

No Huddle boxes have their own assortment of parallels that has small circles running through the background. Blue, Red, Purple, Pink and Neon Green are among the shades with No Huddle exclusives.

First-year players have been a key feature in Prizm’s signature lineups for more than a decade. That continues here with both Rookie Autographs and Rookie Patch Autographs.

Franchise Legends Autographs have ink from some of the NFL’s all-time greats.

Portals is back for a second year. These seamlessly blend photos of players from different parts of their playing careers with a multiverse kind of vibe.

Fireworks returns as well.

Over the last few years, short print inserts have grown. The 2024 Prizm Football checklist has a handful of them including Aurora, Profiles and Prizmania.

Collectors can look for more Japanese art-inspired Manga cards.

There are also more Color Blast and Color Blast Duals.

2024 Panini Prizm Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 12, No Huddle – 8
Packs per box: Hobby – 12, No Huddle – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12, No Huddle – 20
Set size: 400 cards
Release date: December 18, 2024
