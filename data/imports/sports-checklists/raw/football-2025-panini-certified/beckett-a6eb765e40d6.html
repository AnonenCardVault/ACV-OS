Jump to the 2025 Panini Certified Football checklist and team set lists

2025 Panini Certified Football gives collectors what they expect from the long-running brand – plenty of parallels, a solid mix of autographs and memorabilia cards, and the SSP inserts that they love to chase.

Each 10-pack box guarantees two autographs – including one Freshman Fabric Signatures card – two memorabilia cards, and plenty of inserts and parallels.

2025 Panini Certified Football Checklist Overview

The base set features 242 cards, when including the Freshman Fabric Signatures cards with 100 traditional veteran base cards and 100 traditional base rookie cards. The annual staple returns with jumbo swatches and signatures of top rookies from the 2025 NFL Draft.

The base checklist will be chock full of top veterans and rookies, all of which will be covered by a wide-ranging parallel rainbow – including several with the hallmark mirror foil.

The autographs continue in sets like Piece of the Game Signatures, which feature a jersey swatch under a die-cut cutout of the team name.

Panini will also insert the next card from the Patrick Mahomes Autograph Collection into Certified, this time recognizing his league-leading 41 touchdowns from his 2022 MVP season.

Piece of the Game cards also come without autographs, and collectors can find additional memorabilia in other sets like:

  • Certified Ballers
  • Pro Materials
  • Materials Mirror

Inserts complete the package, and collectors will be on the lookout for tough case hits.

Lucky finds include:

  • The Mighty
  • First Round Certifications
  • Night Moves
  • Immortals
