2023 Football Cards, Checklists, New

Jump to the 2023 Panini Certified Football checklist and team set lists.

Although the brand debuted almost 25 years ago, some of the look and feel of 2023 Panini Certified Football will take collectors back to the colorful days of the late 1980s and early ’90s. It continues with a premium leaning offering two autographs and two memorabilia cards in every 10-pack hobby box.

2023 Panini Certified Football Checklist Overview

The main set consists of 200 total cards that are split evenly between current stars and a deep lineup of rookies.

Parallels are three per hobby box, all of which come with serial numbers. They include:

  • Mirror – /349
  • Bronze Mirror – /275
  • Pink Mirror – /199
  • Orange Mirror – /149
  • Red Mirror – /99
  • Blue Mirror – /75
  • Teal Mirror – /50
  • Gold Mirror – /25
  • Purple Mirror – /10
  • Green Mirror – /5
  • Black Mirror – 1/1

Freshman Fabrics Signatures are back, delivering an autograph from one of the season’s first-year standouts alongside a jumbo swatch. Base versions have 399 copies. Parallels come in:

  • Bronze Mirror – /349
  • Pink Mirror – /299
  • Orange Mirror – /249
  • Red Mirror – /199
  • Blue Mirror – /99
  • Teal Mirror – /50
  • Gold Mirror – /25
  • Purple Mirror – /10
  • Green Mirror – /5
  • Black Mirror – 1/1

Additional Autographs, Memorabilia Cards and Other Inserts

Mirror Signatures is one of the places collectors will find autographs from veteran and retired players. Base versions have no more than 199 copies each. Bronze (/99), Pink (/75), Orange (/50), Red (/35), Blue (/30), Teal (/25), Gold (/15), Purple (/10), Green (/5) and Black (1/1) also exist.

Canton Certified Signatures is all Hall of Famers and have a maximum of 149 copies. The Mirror rainbow includes Red (/99), Blue (/75) Teal (/50), Gold (/25), Purple (/10), Green (/5) and Black (1/1).

Fabric of the Game, a longstanding hit in Certified, is one of the product’s memorabilia offerings.

Also on the checklist is Stitches in Time Quad, which has swatches from four players. Etch parallels are available in Pink (/35), Orange (/30), Red (/25), Blue (/20), Teal (/15), Gold (/10), Purple (/5), Green (/3) and Black (1/1).

2023 Panini Certified Football has a revamped insert lineup. Night Moves makes the jump over from Donruss Football.

Quarterbacks take the spotlight in the new Huck It, Chuck It.

2023 is the annual graffiti-themed set highlighting the season’s rookie class. In the past, these have been some of the easier inserts to pull. Now they’re the opposite, falling as one of the product’s tough SSPs.

Another rarity is Radical Rookies, a set that brings the pinks and teals engrained on many minds thanks to decades of Saved by the Bell reruns.
