2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball helps jumpstart the new year of MLB card collecting with another flagship release.

While Topps declined to reveal images in the Retail solicitation, fans can count on a few new options.

*Please note that Topps has not released the Hobby details yet.*

See also: 2023 Topps Series 1 Baseball Cards

2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Base / Parallels

Although the base lineup has offered 330 cards for a few seasons, the 2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball checklist sees the return of the 350-card base set.

The parallels include a familiar assortment along with all-new Yellow, Yellow Foil and Yellow Crackle (#/50) editions exclusive to Hanger boxes.

2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Base Parallel Breakdown

Exclusives noted.

  • Yellow - 2 per box (Hanger)
  • Rainbow Foil - 1:10 packs
  • Royal Blue - 1:10 packs (Retail)
  • Yellow Foil - 1:10 packs (Hanger)
  • Gold - #/2,024
  • Blue Foilboard - #/999
  • Purple Foilboard - #/799
  • Green Foilboard - #/499
  • Orange Foilboard - #/299
  • Red Foilboard - #/199
  • Vintage Stock - #/99
  • Independence Day - #/76
  • Yellow Crackle - #/50 (Hanger)
  • Mother’s Day Hot Pink  - #/50
  • Father’s Day Powder Blue - #/50
  • Memorial Day Camo - #/25
  • Platinum - 1/1
  • Printing Plates - 1/1

Another change comes with the flagship variations. While 2023 featured the Golden Mirror Image Variation set, the 2024 product provides True Photo Variation cards that strip away the design and let the imagery be the focal point.

In addition, spring is in the air for the 2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Value Blasters. Look for exclusive holiday editions that combine to average three per box. These themed cards follow the Halloween cards from 2023 Topps Update Series Baseball.

2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Holiday Base Parallels Breakdown

  • Eggs - 1:21 packs
  • Rabbit - 1:56 packs
  • Flowers - #/50
  • Umbrella - #/10
  • Watering Can - #/5
  • Roses - 1/1

2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Inserts

The 2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball checklist also makes room for many new inserts. 1989 Topps Baseball is home to the latest 35th-anniversary design celebration. The Mega Box format has exclusive 1989 Topps Baseball Foilboard versions with additional foil parallels.

Standouts from the past year are honored in 2023 All Topps Team, for top players, and 2023 Greatest Hits, for top moments.

Other debuts arrive with the Grand Gamers and Superstar Blueprint. The new Topps Mega Stars (Mega Box only) cards add foil parallels.

Limited to 500 copies per card, Heavy Lumber is back to showcase top hitters on a wood-grained design.

Falling once per pack, the 30-card Stars of the MLB set returns as a Retail-exclusive continuation insert also slated for Series 2 and Update Series. Another exclusive, Chrome Stars of the MLB averages 1:10 Retail packs.

2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball Relics / Auto Relics

As for the hits, 2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball sticks with the normal mix, including autographs, relics and autograph relics.

As usual, the throwback 1989 Topps Baseball Autographs line is noted as having on-card autographs.

Chase other signed cards with Baseball Stars Autographs, Baseball Stars Dual Autographs (#/10 or less), Postseason Performance Autographs (#/50 or less), World Series Champions Autographs (#/50 or less), and Cut Signatures (1/1).

The 2024 Topps Series 1 Baseball memorabilia sets include 1989 Baseball Relics, Major League Materials, Postseason Performance Relics (#/99), and World Series Champion Relics (#/99), as well as Major League Material Autographs (#/50 or less), Postseason Performance Autograph Relics (#/50 or less) and World Series Champion Autograph Relics (#/50 or less).

Check back soon for the full set details as revealed by Topps.
