Jump to the 2024 Bowman Baseball checklist and team set lists.

New year, new prospects. 2024 Bowman Baseball has all the autographs, Refractors and up-and-comers synonymous with the brand. With both hobby and retail configurations, there’ll be several ways to chase it.

Traditional hobby boxes once again deliver one autograph while larger jumbo boxes have three.

Bowman Buyback Autographs, which are original cards with added signatures, are also randomly inserted in packs.

Additional Inserts

The insert lineup on the 2024 Bowman Baseball checklist is a mix of familiar and new.

With artificial intelligence being all the rage, Bowman A.I. uses advanced stats to predict future stars.

From the cutting edge in technology to a Happy Days background staple, Prospect Power-Up channels pinball for its design.

Returning inserts include Bowman Scouts’ Top 100, Bowman Spotlights and Rookie of the Year Favorites.

All of these insert sets come with Mini-Diamond Refractors (/150), Aqua Refractors (/125), retail-only Green Refractors (/99), Gold Refractors (/50), Red Refractors (/5) and Superfractors (1/1).

The ultra scarce Hidden Gems cross brand set gets another installment in 2024 Bowman Baseball as well.

2024 Bowman Baseball cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 10, Jumbo – 32, Retail – 12, Blaster – 12
Packs per box: Hobby – 24, Jumbo – 12, Retail – 24, Blaster – 6
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12, Jumbo – 8, Retail – 12, Blaster – 40
Set size: 100 cards
Release date: May 8, 2024
