2024 Panini Absolute Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2024 Football Cards, Checklists

Jump to the 2024 Panini Absolute Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Absolute Football is a product for those who like chasing hits, at least at the hobby level. In recent years, though, the brand has also become home to a couple of the sport’s most popular regular inserts, Kaboom and Explosive. The autographs, the memorabilia cards, the rare inserts — they’re all back.

As far as hobby box guarantee go, there are three autographs in every box including one Rookie Premiere Materials card. Collectors can also expect a pair of memorabilia cards despite hobby boxes coming with just three packs.

2024 Panini Absolute Football Checklist Overview

The main part of the base set has a mix of veterans and rookies. Complimenting them are several levels of parallels including several under the Spectrum banner.

It’s not just single colors, either. Spectrum Splash add something a little extra pop.

Base Autographs are also on the checklist.

The hits headliners are the Rookie Premiere Materials Signatures. These come with several pieces of materials —and not just plain jersey pieces.

Another staple of the brand, Tools of the Trade Signatures, also return. Like the RPM Signatures, these use a variety of relics.

All-Pro Souvenirs is another place to find autographed memorabilia cards.

Kaboom is back with more cartoon-inspired designs. Both vertical and horizontal layouts are available, opening up the checklist to more cards.

Similarly, there’s a new batch of Explosive cards. These take a lot of the same beats as Kaboom including the fact that they’re tough to pull.

2024 Panini Absolute Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 3
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 228 cards
Release date (subject to change): December 27, 2024
