2024 Football Cards, Checklists, New

Jump to the 2024 Panini Illusions Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Illusions Football cards aim to get collectors looking. In addition to a steady flow of autographs and memorabilia cards, the checklist includes some distinct inserts that use a variety of printing technologies.

Hobby boxes have five hits, which average out to one every other pack. Three of these are autographs while the other two are relics.

2024 Panini Illusions Football Checklist Overview

The main shell set consists of 100 cards. Done on holographic stock with a busy design, there’s a lot going on, including the image getting doubled up with a color fully-body shot joined a black and white close up in the background.

Serial-numbered Trophy Collection parallels combine to land five per hobby box. Some cards also have autograph versions.

First Impressions Autographed Memorabilia highlights some of the key names from the season’s rookie class.

Rookie Reflections Dual Patch Autographs have signatures and premium swatches from two players.

Other autographs on the 2024 Illusions checklist include Rookie Signs, Prodigy Endorsements and Elusive Ink, which focuses on players who don’t have a lot of signed cards on the market.

Acetate stock is one of the cornerstones of Illusions’ inserts. The plastic stock can be found in Illusionists, Abracadabra and more.

Holograms are also in the product with new HoloHeroes cards. These are among the tougher cards on the checklist.

2024 Panini Illusions Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 16
Set size: 136 cards
Release date: March 7, 2025
