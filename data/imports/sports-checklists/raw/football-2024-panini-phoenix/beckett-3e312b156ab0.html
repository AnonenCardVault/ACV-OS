Jump to the 2024 Panini Phoenix Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Phoenix Football brings some fire to the hobby’s chrome sub-genre (but no brimstone). Once again delivering a more design-heavy look than releases like Prizm and Select, there’s still the colorful pathways parallels bring.

Hobby boxes deliver three hits including one rookie autographed memorabilia card. Another autograph and a memorabilia card can also be expected.

2024 Panini Phoenix Football Checklist Overview

The base set features both veterans and rookies. It’s chromium so there’s a rainbow of colors and patterns that runs alongside it. Among the parallels are Silver, Phoenix, Red, Wave and Blue Fade. Separate H2 boxes have exclusives like Pandora, Pulsar, Bronze Fade and Gold Pulsar.

Parallels combine to land 12 per hobby box, eight of which have serial numbers. H2 boxes deliver six exclusive parallels.

Some rookies also have autograph versions with additional parallels.

First-year players are in several autographed relic sets like Rookie Phenom Jersey Autographs, Rising Rookie Material Signatures, Rookie Autograph Dual Jersey and Rookies Silhouettes.

Other signature sets include Rising Star Autographs Jersey and Phenomenal Duo Jersey Autographs.

Franchise Future Materials are among the strictly swatch sets.

Phoenix has other standard inserts as well including the return of Contours.

Color Blast, Phoenicians and Gridiron Crowns are the product’s short print chase sets.

2024 Panini Phoenix Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5, H2 – 8
Packs per box: Hobby – 12, H2 – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 16, H2 – 20
Set size: 250 cards
Release date: April 18, 2025
