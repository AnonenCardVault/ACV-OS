Jump to the 2024 Panini Zenith Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Zenith Football is one of those products that has a little bit of a lot of things. Autographs, inserts, parallels and, of course, a base set are all a part of the checklist.

Keeping in line with the past couple of years since the Pinnacle brand was revived, collectors can look for both hobby and retail versions. Hobby is a on the more upscale side with single-pack boxes delivering both an autographed patch and an additional autograph card.

2024 Panini Zenith Football Checklist Overview

The 200-card base set breaks down to 100 veteran players and 100 rookies. Hobby packs typically have one of each or a corresponding parallel. The design takes a more traditional approach versus the past couple of years, opting for something more sleek rather than 1990s bold.

Highlighting the signature chase are Rookie Patch Autographs. Available in a variety of levels and parallels, these come numbered to 399 or fewer.

Other hits include Z-Jersey Autographs and Contenders Optic Preview Tickets, which are numbered to 24 and have both rookie and veteran versions.

While the base set might be a little more elegant and mellow, other inserts embrace bold layouts and designs. Among them are A to Z, Z-Team and Z-Marquee.

2024 Panini Zenith Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 6
Packs per box: Hobby – 1
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 200 cards
Release date: March 21, 2025
