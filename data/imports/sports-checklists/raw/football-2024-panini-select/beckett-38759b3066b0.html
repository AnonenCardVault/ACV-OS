2024 Panini Select Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2024 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New

Jump to the 2024 Panini Select Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Select Football has many levels of color. The staple tiered checklist returns, making for a massive foundation. From there comes a handful of autographs and memorabilia plus several additional inserts. The end result is a massive release that is a challenge no matter how a collector tackles it.

Hobby boxes promise three hits, two of which are autographs. There’s also an average of one or more parallels plus a generous amount of inserts.

2024 Panini Select Football Checklist Overview

Building the entire Select base set requires 500 cards. Big on its own, things are further complicated by the fact that the checklist is broken down into five different levels where rarity varies. Like recent years, each tier has a different design and assortment of parallels.

The parallel rainbow expands with different box configurations. Among them is H2. These have six parallels, four of which are exclusive Disco versions. Colors for these include Red Disco, Blue Disco, Gold Disco, Green Disco and Black Disco.

As far as autographs go, Jumbo Rookie Signature Swatches and Prime Selection Prizm Signatures are among the highlights thanks to their super-sized swatches.

Rookie Signatures Memorabilia also offer an autographed-relic combination.

For those looking to go beyond 2024 draft picks, there’s the self-explanatory Signatures.

Popular Color Wheel inserts are back with a new design.

Other chase sets include Starcade and Watercolors.

Score Select Throwback offers a modern retro vibe.

2025 draft picks appear in 2024 Select Football, albeit not directly. XRC Rookie Redemptions are good for cards that won’t be finalized until next season.

Editor’s Note: Andrew Harner contributed to this article, which was originally published on March 5, 2025.

2024 Panini Select Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5, H2 – 6
Packs per box: Hobby – 12, H2 – 4
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12, H2 – 20
Set size: 500 cards
Release date: May 28, 2025
