Jump to the 2025 Panini Prizm Football checklist and team set lists

As one of the most anticipated products of the football card season, 2025 Panini Prizm Football will again bring everything collectors expect.

From autographs to Prizm parallels to short-printed inserts, each 12-pack box affords collectors many opportunities to find something they love. Collectors should expect to find two autographs in every hobby box.

2025 Panini Prizm Football Checklist Overview

The base set will again run deep, with veterans, legends, and rookies all covered by 72 Prizm parallels, which come in a variety of colors and patterns.

Included are the common Silver Prizms, rare White Knight Prizms, classic Gold Prizms, and some new concepts, such as Kangaroo Prizms, Pigskin Prizms, Lotus Flower Prizms, and Plum Blossom Prizms.

Signed cards from rookies are always a staple of a Prizm set, and this year’s edition returns both Rookie Autographs and Rookie Patch Autographs.

Inserts come as both easy-to-find and difficult-to-find concepts.

Some of the more common themes include Emergent, Prizmatic, and Prizm Break.

On the tough side are popular recurring sets like Prizmania.

Another case hit is Color Blast, which have single and dual versions.

Manga cards also return as SSPs, with both vertical and horizontal variants.
