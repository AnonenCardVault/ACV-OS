2023 Panini Phoenix Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2023 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New, New

Jump to the 2023 Panini Phoenix Football checklist and team set lists.

2023 Panini Phoenix Football brings fire. And ice. And plenty of other color and big designs. The release is one of the bolder pigskin products of the season. Hobby boxes have three hits, two of which are autographs. Lots of inserts and parallels take flight on the checklist.

2023 Panini Phoenix Football Checklist Overview

The base set is anchored by chromium stock and a more modern geometric design. Rather than standing pat with just standard colors to make up the parallel rainbow, collectors can find things like the one-per-hobby-box Color Burst, Lava, Fire and Ice, and the case hit Dream Weaver. More traditional colors are present on the checklist as well like Silver and Red.

Silver parallels are two per hobby box. They also have another 10 that are serial numbered. This works out to an average of one parallel per pack.

H2 boxes are the only place to find Seismic parallels. Colors for these include Red, Purple, Orange, Fire and Ice, Gold and Black.

RPS Rookie Autograph Dual Jersey cards offer signatures and a pair of swatches from key players taken in the 2023 draft.

Mythical Autograph Materials and Paragon Patch Autographs are other places to find signed memorabilia. These both include veteran players.

Standard inserts combine to fall two per hobby box. For those chasing tough cards, there’s En Fuego, Metropolis and Phoenician.

Archetype is another one of the product’s insert sets.

2023 Panini Phoenix Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5, H2 – 8
Packs per box: Hobby – 12, H2 – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 16, H2 – 20
Set size: 200 cards
Release date: Hobby – January 26, 2024, H2 – February 7, 2024

What to expect in a hobby box:

  • Autographs – 2
  • Memorabilia Cards – 1
  • Inserts – 2
  • Color Burst Parallels – 1
  • Silver Parallels – 2
  • Additional Parallels – 10
  • Base Short Prints – 4
  • Rookies – 12

What to expect in a H2 box:

  • Inserts – 9
  • Silver Parallels – 2
  • Additional Parallels – 8
  • Base Short Prints – 3
  • Rookies – 20
