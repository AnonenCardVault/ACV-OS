2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball is the second primary release of the year for Topps' well-known prospect brand.

The popular MLB card line acts as an extension, of sorts, for 2023 Bowman Baseball, but with an all-chromium approach.

Very early set details first appeared via the Topps.com presales for Hobby ($269.99) and HTA Choice ($329.99) boxes on August 15.

The release day box prices on the Topps website increased to $289.99 for Hobby and $365.99 for HTA Choice.

Another option is 2023 Bowman Chrome Mega Box Baseball.

2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball SuperFractor Buyback Program

UPDATE (September 29): After several appeared following the release of the product, Topps confirmed that there are duplicate non-autographed SuperFractors for 95 players in 2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball and revealed a buyback program to purchase one unsigned SuperFractor for each impacted player.

Varying by the player, the buyback prices start at $3,000 and go as high as $75,000 (Ethan Salas).

2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball Inserts

From there, the insert selection for 2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball offers returning lines and new options.

The 2022 AFL Fall Stars (1:6 packs), 2022 AFL MVP Short Print and Bowman Invicta (1:24 packs) sets are all back for 2023, while Bowman Ascensions (1:144 packs) features a collaboration with artist Jeremy Fish.

Debuts include Bowman Iconic (1:3 packs), showcasing the photo from the player's earliest Bowman card on a "glitzy design." Also new, the colorful Prospect Process (1:12 packs) follows the career path for young talent.

Giving collectors a challenge, the super-limited Hidden Gems insert gets another run.
