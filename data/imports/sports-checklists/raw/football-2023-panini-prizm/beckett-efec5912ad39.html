2023 Panini Prizm Football Checklist, Team Set Lists and Details

2023 Football Cards, Checklists, Checklists-New, New

10

Jump to the 2023 Panini Prizm Football checklist and team set lists.

No, 2023 Panini Prizm Football isn’t going to change much of the model given the brand’s juggernaut status in the sport. But that doesn’t mean that there aren’t some notable tweaks and additions.

Autographs remain two per hobby box. Collectors will also find a handful of inserts and 14 Prizms parallels, 10 of which are serial numbered.

2023 Panini Prizm Football Checklist Overview

The core of every Prizm release is its base set. That’s not changing here with a mix of veterans and rookies showcased on chromium stock. It also acts as the launching pad into the expansive parallel rainbow.

Colors and styles available in hobby boxes include:

  • Silver
  • Hyper
  • Orange
  • Blue Wave
  • Purple Ice
  • Red Wave
  • Purple
  • Blue Ice
  • Green Scope
  • Orange Wave
  • Purple Power
  • Navy Camo
  • Forest Camo
  • Gold
  • Gold Vinyl
  • White Knight
  • Black Finite
  • Snakeskin

This list expands with additional exclusive Prizms found in different configurations at both the hobby and retail levels.

The colorful assortment of cards extends to Prizm’s signature selections like Rookie Autographs and Rookie Patch Autographs.

Veteran and retired ink is also available in themes like Franchise Legends.

One of the biggest changes comes with one of the line’s most popular inserts, Color Blast. This time around there are vertical designs as well as Color Blast Duos, which have two players on them.

Manga returns with more art inspired by the Japanese comic style.

The checklist also has a new batch of Hype inserts, amongst others.

2023 Panini Prizm Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 12
Packs per box: Hobby – 12
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 400 cards
Release date: January 3, 2024
