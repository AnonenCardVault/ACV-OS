Jump to the 2024 Panini Mosaic Football checklist and team set lists.

2024 Panini Mosaic Football is one of a handful of chrome sets collectors can expect over the course of the pigskin season. What makes it different from Prizm? Besides the checklist and its expanded base themes, its found in the namesake parallels that add a distinct background pattern to the traditional rainbow.

As far as overall elements go, hobby boxes have two autographs. Collectors can also expect multiple inserts and parallels per pack as well.

2024 Panini Mosaic Football Checklist Overview

Keeping with past Mosaic Football releases, the base set has more than just standard player cards. Additional subsets take specific angles like Hall of Fame.

While regular base cards bring a familiar chromium look and feel, most of the parallels add the Mosaic pattern that adds a repeating tile look. These total 15 per hobby box while more traditional Silver Prizms are five per box. This works out to an average of two parallels per pack.

Among the signature sets is Rookie Autographs, which are based on the main set as far as designs go.

There’s also In Focus Signatures and Showtime Signatures.

Mosaic is traditionally home to a lot of different inserts. It’s no different here. While some are somewhat easy to find, there are also some short prints like Money, Micro Mosaic and Stained Glass.

2024 Panini Mosaic Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 15
Packs per box: Hobby – 10
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 400 cards
Release date: November 27, 2024
