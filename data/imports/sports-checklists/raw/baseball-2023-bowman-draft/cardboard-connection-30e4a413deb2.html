2023 Bowman Draft Baseball is back to highlight recent MLB draft picks along with other notable prospects, including former NFL superstar Tom Brady.

The formats again include Hobby Jumbo boxes with three autographs and Super Jumbo boxes with five autographs. There is also the HTA Choice box with just three cards, all being autographs.

Selling out after several hours, the November 14 presale on the Topps website offered Hobby Jumbo boxes for $479.99 and Super Jumbo boxes for $649.99.

2023 Bowman Draft Baseball is the third release in the main Bowman prospect lineup, following 2023 Bowman Baseball and 2023 Bowman Chrome Baseball.

See also: 2022 Bowman Draft Baseball Cards

2023 Bowman Draft Baseball Base / Parallels

Working in 200 names, the 2023 Bowman Draft Baseball checklist offers a base set with "1st Bowman" notations and multiple Refractor editions.

On the base paper side, Aqua/Pink Gradient Border (#/199) and Red/Green Gradient (#/5) are new options, while Chrome fans have new Lunar and Rose Gold editions

A total of 10 top names from the base set are given Image Variation short prints, while all-time legends Carl Yastrzemski and Josh Gibson join the RetroFractor ranks. These are all noted as being "very limited."

2023 Bowman Draft Baseball Inserts

Adding new lines to complete, the 2023 Bowman Draft Baseball inserts include Bowman 1954 Flag Variation, Bowman Glass and the limited Mood Ring (1:5 cases).

Highlighting young talent, Draft Picks & Prospects goes with a Dungeons & Dragons design style.

The eye-catching Transformative Talent amps up the electricity generated by budding superstars.

Another chase is the 1995 Bowman Dream Draft Picks insert, showcasing Tom Brady during his MLB prospect days with the Montreal Expos.

There are limited autograph versions numbered to 50 copies or less and some have inscriptions.

2023 Bowman Draft Baseball Autographs

From there, the 2023 Bowman Draft Baseball checklist makes plenty of room for more autographs.

The Chrome Prospect Autographs series covers 101 players hoping to make their mark at the MLB level.

Several of the inserts are treated to autographs via Bowman 1954 Flag Variation Red Autographs (#/5), Draft Picks & Prospects Autographs (#/99) and Mood Ring Red Autographs (#/5).

Other signed options include Bowman Draft Night Autographs (#/99), Class of 2023 Autographs and Franchise Futures Dual Autographs (#/99).

2023 Bowman Draft Baseball also has book cards to chase with Picked for Excellence Autograph Booklet (#/5) and Positioned for Excellence Autograph Booklet (#/10).

On top of that, the Under Armour All-America Game Autographs cards return with another batch of key prospects.

Release Date: December 12, 2023
Hobby Jumbo Configuration: 32 cards per pack, 12 packs per box, 8 boxes per case
Super Jumbo Configuration: 120 cards per pack, 5 packs per box, 6 boxes per case
HTA Choice Configuration: 3 cards per pack, 1 pack per box, 6 boxes per case
