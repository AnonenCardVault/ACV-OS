Jump to the 2025 Panini Absolute Football checklist and team set lists

2025 Panini Absolute Football will deliver on just about everything collectors love. From a comprehensive base set with dozens of parallels to autographs and memorabilia hits to rare case-hit inserts, the product is always a delight for collectors to open.

Though there are only 15 cards spread across three packs in every hobby box, Panini guarantees five hits – one Rookie Premiere Materials Autograph, two additional autographs, and two memorabilia cards.

2025 Panini Absolute Football Checklist Overview

The base set will combine veteran stars and rookies, and Panini will cover it in a variety of colorful parallels, including the popular Spectrum versions.

Collectors will find six base cards, one rookie card, and two parallels in every hobby box.

As always, memorabilia relic cards play a key role in Absolute Football, a product that debuted as Playoff Absolute Memorabilia in 2001.

Concepts in 2025 will include:

  • Tools of the Trade
  • Absolute Heroes
  • Ground Hoggs

In addition to Rookie Premiere Materials Autographs, collectors can find more ink in sets like All-Pro Souvenir Signatures.

Panini will also insert the next card from the Patrick Mahomes Autograph Collection into Absolute, this time recognizing his second Super Bowl MVP honor.

Kaboom inserts and parallels will again be a big chase for collectors ripping into packs and boxes of Absolute.

In addition to the Horizontal and Vertical versions that have been commonplace since 2022, Panini has introduced Super Bowl Kaboom cards and Purple parallels.

Glass inserts and their 1/1 parallels also return.

2025 Panini Absolute Football cards at a glance:

Cards per pack: Hobby – 5
Packs per box: Hobby – 3
Boxes per case: Hobby – 12
Set size: 200 cards
Release date (subject to change): October 31, 2025
